About Me

Hi,

I'm Tathagata, a photographer based in Kolkata.

I'm not a professional, but photography is a passion that brings me immense joy. What started as a hobby has become a journey of exploration and learning, as I delve into various niches like Wildlife, Nature, and Landscape.

I'm fascinated by the ability of photography to capture fleeting moments, evoke emotions, and tell stories. I strive to use my lens to showcase the beauty of the world around me, from the grand landscapes to the intricate details in nature. Through my photos, I hope to share my perspective and inspire others to see the world through a creative lens.

I'm constantly learning and evolving as a photographer, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me. I'm open to new challenges and collaborations, so feel free to contact me if you have any inquiries or projects in mind.

You can also follow my work on social media at ttgt.photo.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
